Welcome to Maine Menu Match, our new tool to help readers and fans of Maine’s restaurant scene find their next favorite eating spot.

Want a place that’s kid-friendly? A restaurant that has vegetarian options? A unique spot for a special occasion? Answer a few questions from our chatbot and check out the suggestions at the end, based on the parameters you set.

Maine Menu Match isn’t replacing the expertise and talent of our writers, who have spent hundreds of hours in research, writing and tasting at restaurants and eateries in Greater Portland and beyond. This tool is simply combing through our published articles to deliver answers.

Maine Menu Match also has limits. Since it’s not human and doesn’t actually have an opinion on what’s good, it sometimes might not have specific answers to your questions, but it will take a guess and offer you some options. Who knows? You might discover some unexpected dining gems you hadn’t known about before. Just be sure to check the restaurant’s website for the most up-to-date information.

Maine Menu Match uses AI to help to process the restaurants we’ve written about to match your questions with recommendations from the food team’s history of restaurant news and reviews. No information that you enter is used to train or otherwise augment generative AI models.
